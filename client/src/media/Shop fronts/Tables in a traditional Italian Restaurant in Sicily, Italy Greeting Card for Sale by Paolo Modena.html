Product Details

Our greeting cards are 5" x 7" in size and are produced on digital offset printers using 100 lb. paper stock. Each card is coated with a UV protectant on the outside surface which produces a semi-gloss finish. The inside of each card has a matte white finish and can be customized with your own message up to 500 characters in length. Each card comes with a white envelope for mailing or gift giving.

About Paolo Modena

If you love Italy and you love elegance, you have just found the perfect combination. I like to contribute elegance to your everyday life using photographic creations designed for emotional impact. My name is Paolo Modena, and I bring over two decades of experience in photography with me. I passionately work to share the beauty of Italian Art with you. I live in a country surrounded by the most exquisite art and I share this experience through my photography.
